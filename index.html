About C³

A community built to empower.

The Cloud Community Club (C³) is a student-driven initiative at SNIST. We exist to tear down the wall between theoretical college knowledge and actual, hands-on application.

We don't just study technology // we deploy it.
We specialize in aggressive, real-world engineering through Cloud, applied AI, and Open Source integration.

This is your platform to innovate, collaborate, and build the future!

Not just a timeline.
A continuous orbit.

Officially established C³ at SNIST with a founding team and faculty advisor. The base environment was successfully configured.

A national AI hackathon with 250+ participants and 80+ project submissions. Industry mentors, incredible energy, and flawless execution.

Inaugural Open Session
